Ingredients:
  - 1 cup cooked hot white rice
  - 1 egg
  - Soy sauce
  - Kosher salt
  - Mirin
  - Hondashi
  - Furikake

Directions:
  1. Pain rice in a bowl and make a shallow indentation in the center
  2. Break the whole egg into the center.
  3. Season with 1/2 teaspoon soy sauce and a pinch of salt.
  4. Add Mrin and pinch of Hondashi
  5. Stir vigorously with chopsticks to incorporate egg
